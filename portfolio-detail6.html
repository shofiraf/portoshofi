Traincal Fitness App

UI/UX Design

Traincal is a native iOS App that solves how to perform the correct basic movement of calisthenic by create movement analysis app and turn it into beginner calisthenics programs. This is a solution to the challenge because users want to know correct movement of calisthenic in order to maximize their gain and prevent injury .

Category:

iOS App

Company or Client:

Apple Developer Academy @BINUS

Location:

Jakarta, Indonesia

Project Date:

2021

Project Background

From our research, we concluded some information that most users (18 - 24 years old) are familiar with calisthenic exercise. Calisthenic is the best solution for workout needs at home because it can be done just about anywhere and anytime, little to no equipment, and covers a wide range of exercises.

Feature in Traincal App Project

  • Real-Time Movement Analysis -- TrainCal’s AI detects and corrects your form in real-time, ensuring proper technique for foundational calisthenics moves like push-ups, pull-ups, and squats—preventing injuries and maximizing gains.
  • Personalized Beginner Programs -- Generates tailored workout plans based on your movement analysis, adapting to your skill level with progressive exercises to build strength and mobility.
  • iOS-Optimized Experience -- A sleek, intuitive native app with motion-sensor integration for precise feedback, designed for iPhone’s advanced hardware.
